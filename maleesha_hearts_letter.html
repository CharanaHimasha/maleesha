Once upon a time, there was a boy who was honestly too soft-hearted for this world. He didn't focus much on studies the way everyone expected him to. Instead, he spent his time learning about life in his own way — researching, exploring different fields, understanding people, technology, emotions, and the world itself. He gained knowledge from many places, but he failed to learn one important thing: how to survive in society and how to truly belong in the adult world.
Because of that, he trusted people too easily. He believed others would care the same way he cared for them. But life wasn't always kind to him. Slowly, disappointment after disappointment made him tired of people. So he closed his heart, buried his emotions deep inside, and started walking a rough path — where he convinced himself that success, achievements, and qualifications were the only things that mattered.
Then one day, while he was at the office, he noticed a simple girl sitting quietly at a desk, waiting for an interview. At that moment, he never imagined that this pretty little girl would someday completely change the life of a boy who had almost forgotten how to feel human again.
Days passed. At an office party, while everyone else was enjoying themselves, the boy was still busy working. Eventually, after a lot of pressure, he finally stopped for a while and joined the event.
And then… while people were singing, he heard a beautiful voice beside him softly singing along. That's when he realized — it was the same girl he had seen sitting at that desk the other day.
At first, he simply thought, "Oh… nice. Someone who genuinely knows how to enjoy life."
But without realizing it, that small moment taught him something important: no matter how hard life gets, happiness matters too. Enjoying little moments matters too.
After that, the boy slowly started opening his heart to the girl. Not suddenly, not perfectly — but little by little, with understanding, comfort, and trust.
And maybe you won't believe this… but within just a few months, they created memories worth a lifetime.
That's when the boy realized something. This girl wasn't "just a girl."
She was a princess. A girl who could find happiness in the smallest things, who carried warmth everywhere she went, and who unknowingly brought life back into someone who thought his heart had already gone cold.
And now, they're still here… still laughing, still arguing, still making memories, and still planning their next adventure together.
And the funniest part is… that boy still doesn't fully realize when exactly she became such an important part of his life.
But somehow, she did. Not only in his life, but even in his family too. 🤍
